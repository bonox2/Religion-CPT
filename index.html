Who is involved?

Many members of faith communities able to complete a pilgrimage. It's not just Catholics, because in other faiths people also do this.

What people do?

Simply put, a pilgrimage is a spiritual journey. People are totally immersed in prayer and want to get closer to God with people who think the same way.

Why people do?

The Catholic Catechism recognizes the value of pilgrimage as an opportunity for Catholics to work on their faith. Pilgrimages evoke our earthly journey towards heaven.
